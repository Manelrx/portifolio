## What is the Hackers do Bem Program?

The "Hackers do Bem" (Hackers for Good) program is a strategic initiative by the Brazilian government, in collaboration with major companies and institutions, created to address the shortage of qualified cybersecurity professionals in the country and strengthen the digital security ecosystem. It is considered the largest training program in the field, offering comprehensive and accessible education for all.

## Program Objectives and Structure

The main goal of Hackers do Bem is to train a large number of individuals, from beginners to those seeking specialization, for careers in the cybersecurity field. The program structure was designed to be inclusive and comprehensive:

* **Quality Education:** Offers a curriculum ranging from basic to advanced levels, with relevant and up-to-date content.
* **Strategic Partnerships:** Features participation from renowned entities such as the National Research and Education Network (RNP), SENAI-SP, and Softex, ensuring teaching quality and applicability.
* **Completely Free:** All access to courses and materials is 100% free, removing financial barriers to participation.

## Who Can Participate and How Does It Work?

The Hackers do Bem program is democratic and open to a wide audience, requiring no prior knowledge of cybersecurity. Students, IT professionals, or professionals from other fields looking to transition into the sector can enroll. The learning journey typically includes:

1.  **Registration:** Sign up through the program's official website.
2.  **Training Modules:** Access to online courses that progress in complexity.
3.  **Hands-on Activities:** Theory and practice-based learning, often using lab environments.
4.  **Events and Community:** Participation in events and the opportunity to join a professional network in the field.
5.  **Certification:** Obtaining a certificate upon completing the program stages.

## Why is Hackers do Bem Important?

In an increasingly digital world, cybersecurity becomes fundamental for the protection of data, systems, and people. Hackers do Bem plays a crucial role by:

* **Developing Talent:** Directly contributes to reducing the cybersecurity professional shortage in the Brazilian market.
* **Strengthening National Security:** By qualifying more people, it increases the country's capacity to defend against cyber threats.
* **Promoting Inclusion:** Offers a valuable opportunity for free professional qualification, democratizing access to a high-demand field.

The program not only trains individuals but also drives the development of the cybersecurity industry in Brazil.

For more information and to register, visit: [https://hackersdobem.org.br/](https://hackersdobem.org.br/).
